# Kho kien thuc tai chinh ca nhan Viet Nam

## 1. Quy tac tai chinh co ban

### Quy tac 50/30/20

- 50% thu nhap: nhu cau thiet yeu (nha, an uong, di lai, hoa don)
- 30% thu nhap: mong muon (giai tri, mua sam, du lich)
- 20% thu nhap: tiet kiem va dau tu
- Dieu chinh theo thu nhap thuc te: thu nhap thap co the la 70/20/10

### Quy du phong

- Toi thieu 6 thang chi tieu co dinh
- Giu trong tai khoan tiet kiem de rut khi can (khong khoa)
- Uu tien xay quy du phong TRUOC khi dau tu
- Thu nhap 20tr → chi tieu 15tr/thang → quy du phong = 90tr

### Tra no thong minh

- Tra no lai suat cao truoc (phuong phap Avalanche)
- Thu tu uu tien: the tin dung (2-4%/thang) > vay tieu dung (1-2%/thang) > vay mua nha (0.6-0.8%/thang)
- Khong bao gio chi tra muc toi thieu the tin dung (lai kep se tang nhanh)
- Loi khuyen: het no lai cao → lap quy du phong → bat dau dau tu

## 2. Cac kenh dau tu pho bien tai Viet Nam

### Gui tiet kiem ngan hang

- Lai suat: 4-6%/nam (2025)
- Rui ro: rat thap (duoc bao hiem tien gui den 125 trieu/ngan hang)
- Thanh khoan: cao (rut truoc han mat lai)
- Phu hop: quy du phong, nguoi khong chap nhan rui ro
- Meo: gui tiet kiem online lai suat cao hon quay 0.5-1%

### Chung chi quy trai phieu

- Vi du: TCBF (Techcombank), VCBF-FI (Vietcombank)
- Lai suat ky vong: 7-9%/nam
- Rui ro: thap
- Thoi gian: 1-3 nam
- Phu hop: tien nhan roi 6 thang - 2 nam, muon cao hon gui tiet kiem

### Chung chi quy co phieu

- Vi du: VFMVN30, SSIAM VNFIN Lead, VinaCapital VEOF
- Lai suat ky vong: 10-15%/nam (dai han 5-10 nam)
- Rui ro: trung binh - cao (co the giam 20-30% trong nam xau)
- Thoi gian: 3-5 nam tro len
- Phu hop: nguoi chap nhan rui ro, dau tu dai han, ap dung DCA

### Co phieu

- Mua qua cong ty chung khoan (SSI, VNDirect, VCSC, Mirae Asset)
- Lai suat ky vong: 12-20%/nam (nhung rui ro cao)
- Rui ro: cao (co the mat 50%+ trong bear market)
- Can kien thuc: phan tich co ban, phan tich ky thuat
- Phu hop: nguoi co kien thuc, thoi gian theo doi, chap nhan rui ro

### Vang

- Hinh thuc: vang mieng SJC, vang nhan, ETF vang
- Vai tro: phong ve lam phat, da dang hoa danh muc
- Phan bo khuyen nghi: 5-10% danh muc
- Rui ro: bien dong gia ngan han, chenh lech mua-ban cao (vang mieng)

### Trai phieu chinh phu

- Lai suat: 2-4%/nam (thap)
- Rui ro: rat thap nhat (chinh phu bao lanh)
- Phu hop: nguoi cuc ky an toan, to chuc

## 3. Thuat ngu tai chinh

### Lai suat kep (Compound Interest)

Lai tinh tren ca goc va lai da tich luy. Vi du: 100tr lai 10%/nam

- Nam 1: 100tr + 10tr = 110tr
- Nam 2: 110tr + 11tr = 121tr (lai tinh tren 110tr, khong phai 100tr)
- Nam 10: ~259tr (gap 2.59 lan goc)
  Quy tac 72: 72 / lai suat = so nam de gap doi tien. VD: lai 8% → 72/8 = 9 nam gap doi.

### Lam phat (Inflation)

Muc tang gia chung cua hang hoa. Viet Nam trung binh 3-4%/nam.

- 1 trieu dong nam nay ≈ 960K suc mua nam sau (lam phat 4%)
- Dau tu phai danh bai lam phat de giu gia tri tien
- Gui tiet kiem 5% - lam phat 4% = lai thuc chi 1%

### P/E Ratio

Price / Earnings per share. He so gia / loi nhuan.

- P/E = 15: ban bo 15 dong de mua 1 dong loi nhuan/nam
- P/E cao: thi truong ky vong tang truong (hoac dang dat)
- P/E thap: re (hoac dang gap kho khan)
- VN-Index P/E trung binh: 12-16

### ROI (Return on Investment)

Ty suat sinh loi = (Loi nhuan - Chi phi) / Chi phi x 100%

- Mua 100tr co phieu, ban 130tr → ROI = 30%
- Dung de so sanh hieu qua cac kenh dau tu

### DCA (Dollar Cost Averaging)

Dau tu deu dan hang thang bat ke gia thi truong.

- VD: mua 5tr chung chi quy moi thang
- Gia cao → mua it don vi, gia thap → mua nhieu don vi
- Trung binh hoa gia mua, giam rui ro timing

### Diversification (Da dang hoa)

Khong bo tat ca trung vao 1 gio.

- Phan bo: tiet kiem + trai phieu + co phieu + vang
- Phan bo theo do tuoi: (100 - tuoi)% vao co phieu, con lai vao trai phieu/tiet kiem
- Vi du 30 tuoi: 70% co phieu/quy, 30% tiet kiem/trai phieu

## 4. So sanh kenh dau tu theo profile

### Thu nhap duoi 15 trieu/thang

- Uu tien: quy du phong (gui tiet kiem) → tra no (neu co)
- Dau tu nho: 500K-2tr/thang vao chung chi quy trai phieu
- Khong nen: co phieu (chua co kien thuc + von nho)

### Thu nhap 15-30 trieu/thang

- Uu tien: quy du phong 90-180tr → bat dau dau tu
- Phan bo: 60% tiet kiem/trai phieu, 40% chung chi quy co phieu
- Bat dau DCA 3-5tr/thang vao quy
- Co the xem xet: 1-2 co phieu blue chip (neu co kien thuc)

### Thu nhap tren 30 trieu/thang

- Uu tien: da dang hoa danh muc
- Phan bo: 40% co phieu/quy co phieu, 30% trai phieu/quy trai phieu, 20% tiet kiem, 10% vang
- Co the xem xet: bat dong san (neu du von), ETF quoc te

## 5. Luu y quan trong

- Moi thong tin chi mang tinh tham khao, khong phai tu van dau tu chuyen nghiep
- Lai suat qua khu khong dam bao tuong lai
- Luon tim hieu ky truoc khi dau tu
- Khong vay tien de dau tu
- Khong dau tu vao thu minh khong hieu
- Canh giac voi cac mo hinh hua lai suat cao bat thuong (>20%/nam khong rui ro = lua dao)
