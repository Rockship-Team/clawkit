# Case template: SME B2B — sales/ops role

**Match khi nào:** receiver la sales lead / sales manager / ops manager / COO / founder cua mot SME B2B (5-100 nguoi).

## Profile

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Loai doanh nghiep | SME B2B |
| Team / vai tro | sales / ops |
| Cong viec con thu cong | follow-up khach hang, cap nhat CRM, tong hop bao cao |
| Pain chinh | ton thoi gian, de sot deal, kho scale khi pipeline lon |
| LogicX support | dua AI vao workflow van hanh (khong phai chatbot / content) |

## Goc mo email (ƯU TIÊN dung khi research khong tim duoc signal rieng)

> "Gan day toi thay nhieu team SME da thu AI cho content / chatbot, nhung cac phan viec van hanh nhu follow-up, cap nhat CRM, tong hop bao cao van con phai lam tay."

Day la pain pattern #4 trong 4 pattern fallback — nhung **uu tien template nay khi receiver match SME B2B sales/ops profile**.

## Bridge cau noi (LogicX approach)

> "Team LogicX dang lam theo huong: gan AI truc tiep vao nhung workflow nay — follow-up tu dong sau N ngay, cap nhat CRM tu email/cuoc goi, tong hop bao cao tu data raiac."

KHONG mention "marketing automation" / "chatbot" / "AI generic" — keo lech trong tam.

## Soft CTA

> "Neu phu hop, toi co the gui anh/chi 1-2 vi du ngan ve cach mot vai SME B2B da trien khai de tham khao."

KHONG dung "case study" tu trang trong (sounds salesy) — dung "vi du ngan".

## Subject goi y (3-5 variant, KHONG clickbait)

- "Cau hoi ve workflow van hanh — LogicX"
- "AI cho phan viec con lam tay"
- "Follow-up + CRM + bao cao — automate the?"
- "Quan sat ve operational tasks"

## Vi du email full theo template

```
Chao anh {first_name},

Gan day toi thay nhieu team SME da thu AI cho content hoac chatbot, nhung cac phan viec van hanh nhu follow-up khach, cap nhat CRM, tong hop bao cao thi van con phai lam tay — ton kha nhieu thoi gian va de sot deal.

Team LogicX dang lam theo huong: gan AI truc tiep vao nhung workflow nay, de tu dong follow-up sau N ngay, dong bo data tu email/cuoc goi vao CRM, va tong hop bao cao theo tuan tu data raiac.

Neu phu hop, toi co the gui anh 1-2 vi du ngan ve cach mot vai SME B2B da trien khai de tham khao.

Tran trong,
{Sender_Name}
LogicX
```

Body length: ~85 words (in 60-110 range).

## Khi NAO override template nay

- Neu research tim duoc signal cu the rieng cho receiver (vd company hiring sales 5 nguoi, founder share post ve scale ops, CEO interview podcast) → uu tien signal rieng do, KHONG dung goc mo "nhieu team da thu AI" generic.
- Neu receiver KHONG match SME B2B profile (vd D2C consumer brand, Enterprise 1000+ nguoi, agency creative) → dung template khac (TBD: them references khac trong folder nay).
