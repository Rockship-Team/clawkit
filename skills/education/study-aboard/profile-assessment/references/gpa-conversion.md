# GPA Conversion & Profile Scoring Reference

## Vietnamese GPA (thang 10) → US GPA (thang 4.0)

| Thang 10 (VN) | Thang 4.0 (US) | Xếp loại VN | Xếp loại US |
|---------------|---------------|-------------|-------------|
| 9.0 – 10.0    | 3.9 – 4.0     | Xuất sắc    | A / A+      |
| 8.5 – 8.9     | 3.7 – 3.8     | Giỏi        | A-          |
| 8.0 – 8.4     | 3.5 – 3.6     | Giỏi        | B+ / A-     |
| 7.5 – 7.9     | 3.2 – 3.4     | Khá         | B+          |
| 7.0 – 7.4     | 2.8 – 3.1     | Khá         | B / B+      |
| 6.5 – 6.9     | 2.5 – 2.7     | Trung bình  | B-          |
| 6.0 – 6.4     | 2.0 – 2.4     | Trung bình  | C+          |
| < 6.0         | < 2.0         | Yếu         | C hoặc thấp hơn |

> **Lưu ý context quan trọng:**
> - GPA từ trường chuyên (Lê Hồng Phong, Ams, Năng khiếu...) thường bị mark-down 0.1–0.3 điểm khi chuyển đổi vì độ khó cao hơn.
> - Trường quốc tế (IB, A-Level, AP) có thang điểm riêng — xem bên dưới.
> - Nếu học sinh không biết weighted vs unweighted GPA: hỏi rõ trường có cộng điểm cho AP/IB không.

## IB (International Baccalaureate) Score Guide

| IB Total Points | Tương đương US GPA | Nhận xét |
|-----------------|-------------------|----------|
| 40 – 45         | 4.0               | Exceptional |
| 36 – 39         | 3.8 – 3.9         | Very strong |
| 32 – 35         | 3.5 – 3.7         | Strong |
| 28 – 31         | 3.2 – 3.4         | Competitive |
| 24 – 27         | 2.8 – 3.1         | Average |
| < 24            | < 2.8             | Below competitive for top schools |

IB Diploma completion = signal of rigor; Predicted IB score is often used for early application review.

## AP Score Weighting

| AP Score | Nhận xét |
|----------|----------|
| 5        | College credit at most schools; shows mastery |
| 4        | Strong; many schools award credit |
| 3        | Passing; limited credit value for top schools |
| 1–2      | Not competitive; omit from application unless required |

Number of AP courses with scores ≥ 4 is a strong **course rigor** signal.

## Profile Scorecard — Scoring Rubric

### Academics (0–10)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 9–10  | GPA ≥ 3.9 (US) / 9.0+ (VN) từ trường chuyên hoặc quốc tế; hoặc IB ≥ 40 |
| 7–8   | GPA 3.5–3.8; course rigor cao (AP/IB/A-Level); xếp hạng lớp top 10% |
| 5–6   | GPA 3.0–3.4; một số AP; xếp hạng lớp top 25% |
| 3–4   | GPA 2.5–2.9; ít hoặc không có AP/IB |
| 1–2   | GPA < 2.5; không có course rigor |

### Test Scores (0–10)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 9–10  | SAT ≥ 1500 hoặc ACT ≥ 34; TOEFL ≥ 110 hoặc IELTS ≥ 7.5 |
| 7–8   | SAT 1400–1490 hoặc ACT 31–33; TOEFL 100–109 hoặc IELTS 7.0 |
| 5–6   | SAT 1300–1390 hoặc ACT 28–30; TOEFL 90–99 hoặc IELTS 6.5 |
| 3–4   | SAT 1200–1290 hoặc ACT 25–27; TOEFL 80–89 |
| 1–2   | SAT < 1200 hoặc chưa thi |
| N/A   | Chưa thi bất kỳ bài thi nào — không tính điểm, ghi nhận để ưu tiên |

### Extracurriculars (0–10)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 9–10  | ≥ 1 Tier 1 activity + ≥ 2 Tier 2 activities; clear narrative spike |
| 7–8   | ≥ 2 Tier 2 activities; some Tier 1 indicators |
| 5–6   | Mostly Tier 3; 1 Tier 2 activity; reasonable breadth |
| 3–4   | All Tier 3–4; no leadership, no external recognition |
| 1–2   | Minimal ECs; nothing above Tier 4 |

### Essay Readiness (0–10)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 9–10  | Final draft submitted; strong voice; no AI flags |
| 7–8   | 2nd or 3rd draft; improving across revisions |
| 5–6   | 1st draft submitted; clear story but needs polish |
| 3–4   | Outline only; story is clear but underdeveloped |
| 1–2   | Brainstorm only or no essay started |
| N/A   | Not yet started (mark N/A, not 0, to avoid penalizing early students) |

### Overall Profile (0–10)

Weighted average — weight by admissions emphasis:

| Component | Weight |
|-----------|--------|
| Academics | 30% |
| Test Scores | 20% |
| Extracurriculars | 25% |
| Essay Readiness | 25% |

Round to 1 decimal. Report as X/10.

## Reach / Target / Safety Classification

| Category | Admission probability | When to assign |
|----------|-----------------------|----------------|
| Reach    | < 25%                 | Acceptance rate < 25%, or student is below 25th percentile of admitted range |
| Target   | 25–65%                | Student is within or near median admitted stats range |
| Safety   | ≥ 70%                 | Student is clearly above median; rolling admissions schools often qualify |

> For **international students**: acceptance rates are generally 30–50% lower than the published overall rate. Adjust Reach/Target accordingly. A school with 30% overall rate may be Reach for international applicants.
