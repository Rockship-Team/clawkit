# Pricing Strategy Guide

## Package Matching Logic

### When to Recommend Starter
- Client budget < 20M VND/năm
- Small team (1-5 people)
- Single channel use case
- Testing/validation phase
- Limited scale (< 1,000 transactions/month)

### When to Recommend Pro
- Client budget 100-500M VND/năm
- Medium team (5-20 people)
- Multi-channel requirements
- Established business
- Moderate scale (1,000-50,000 transactions/month)

### When to Recommend Enterprise
- Client budget > 500M VND/năm
- Large team (20+ people)
- Complex integrations needed
- Enterprise requirements
- High scale (> 50,000 transactions/month)
- On-premise or hybrid deployment

## Negotiation Tips

### Value-Based Positioning
- Focus on ROI, not cost
- Show case studies with similar companies
- Emphasize unique Vietnamese language capabilities
- Highlight Zalo integration advantage
- Stress local support availability

### Common Objections & Responses
- "Too expensive": Show 3-month ROI breakdown
- "Competitor cheaper": Compare feature gap, support quality
- "Need trial": Offer 14-day trial with limited features
- "Budget cuts": Offer payment plan, phased rollout

### Closing Techniques
- Anchor high → compromise down
- Scarcity: "Price increases April 1st"
- Social proof: "Company X just signed"
- Trial close: "Does this work for you?"

## Contract Terms

- Payment: 50% upfront, 50% on go-live (Enterprise: monthly)
- Term: 12 months minimum
- Auto-renewal: Yes (90-day notice to cancel)
- Price increase: Max 5% per year
- Termination fee: 3 months remaining service
