### SAT Notes

- Score delivery: approximately 13 days after test date.

- Score Choice: students can choose which scores to send; most schools practice Score Choice or superscore.
- SAT School Day (school-administered): available in Spring — check with school directly.

## Deadline → Test Date Mapping

Use this when recommending a test date based on the student's earliest EA/ED deadline.

| Application Deadline | Latest SAT/ACT to sit | Register by |
|----------------------|-----------------------|-------------|
| Nov 1 (ED/EA Round 1) | Oct 4, 2025 SAT / Oct 25, 2025 ACT | Early Sep 2025 |
| Nov 15 | Oct 4, 2025 SAT / Oct 25, 2025 ACT | Early Sep 2025 |
| Jan 1 (RD / EA II) | Dec 6, 2025 SAT / Dec 13, 2025 ACT | Early Nov 2025 |
| Rolling / Mar 1 | Mar 14, 2026 SAT / Feb 7, 2026 ACT | Mid-Jan 2026 |

**Backup rule**: If the recommended sitting is unavailable or the student isn't ready, advise the next available date and check whether it still allows scores to arrive before the deadline (most schools accept scores reported by test day even if official report arrives slightly after).

## Score Sending Timeline

- SAT scores: sent electronically within ~2 weeks of score release. Most schools accept scores reported by test day (i.e., student doesn't need scores in hand before deadline).
- ACT scores: same policy.
- Rush reporting: College Board charges extra (~$31) for rush score delivery — usually not necessary if test is ≥ 3 weeks before deadline.

## TOEFL / IELTS Key Dates

TOEFL and IELTS run nearly every week — use `references/toefl-breakdown.md` for planning. General guidance:

- Allow **at least 3 weeks** between last TOEFL sitting and EA/ED deadline for scores to arrive.
- IELTS scores: available in ~13 days; paper-based IELTS in ~13–28 days.
- Duolingo English Test: instant results, accepted by most US universities as of 2024–2025.
