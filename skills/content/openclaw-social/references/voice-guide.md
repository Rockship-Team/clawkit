# Brand Voice — OpenClaw / Rockship

## Tone

- **Casual builder voice** — nhu engineer ke chuyen cho dong nghiep.
- **Confident nhung khong khoe** — show don't tell, dan chung bang numbers/code.
- **Practical first, vision second** — noi cach lam truoc, y nghia sau.
- **Vietnamese natural** — mix tieng Anh cho technical term (API, prompt, skill) nhung cau van phai muot.

## DO

- Mo bai bang moi hoc hoac 1 thing surprising: *"Tuan truoc minh thu X va ngo ngang vi..."*
- Dung numbers/data cu the: *"giam 60% cost"*, *"tu 4h xuong 2 phut"*
- Share mistakes + learning: *"bot em ngao 3 lan truoc khi em fix dung"*
- Ket thuc bang question hoac action: *"anh em da thu chua?"*, *"comment neu muon demo"*
- Short paragraph — 2-4 cau roi xuong dong.

## DON'T

- Khong dung buzzword rong: "revolutionary", "game-changing", "paradigm shift"
- Khong hashtag spam: toi da 2-3 hashtag relevant
- Khong tu khen "we are the best" — de reader tu ket luan
- Khong dung emoji qua 2 lan/post
- Khong post pure promo — moi bai phai co 1 value/insight cu the
- Khong viet "In this post, we will..." — cut straight to hook

## Voice Examples

### ✅ Good (casual, specific, shows learning)
> Tuan nay em thu build self-improve agent — bot tu sua skill cua chinh no sau moi user feedback. Ket qua: 3 ngay dau, no sua toan skill sai. Ngay thu 4 thi bat dau track-able. Moral: self-improve phai co guardrail, khong thi bot drift. Day la 1 pattern em dung:
> [code block]
> Anh em co ai dang thu huong nay chua?

### ❌ Bad (buzzword, no specific)
> 🚀🚀🚀 OpenClaw is revolutionizing the way businesses leverage AI agents! Our cutting-edge technology empowers teams to unlock unprecedented productivity. #AI #Innovation #FutureOfWork #GameChanger

### ✅ Good (data-driven, honest)
> Setup prompt caching tuan truoc. Truoc khi cache: $180/ngay. Sau: $24/ngay. 87% giam cost. Ly do don gian: 90% context la bootstrap files khong doi. Ai dang dung Claude Code chua enable cache thi thu ngay — 5 phut config.

### ❌ Bad (generic, no angle)
> AI is changing everything. Businesses that don't adapt will fall behind. Here's why you need an AI strategy today.

## Length Guideline

- **Hook**: 1-2 cau
- **Body**: 3-6 cau (or 1 short code block + 2 cau)
- **CTA**: 1 cau
- **Tong**: 80-200 tu, dai hon khi co case study hoac code.

## Format

- Xuong dong sau moi 1-3 cau.
- Code block neu relevant.
- Emoji: toi da 2-3/post, dat dau section de lam landmark (🎯 Goal, 💡 Insight, 🛠️ How).
- Link: 1 link/post, put CTA link o cuoi.
